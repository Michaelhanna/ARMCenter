At the "ARM" Archangel Raphael Ministry Center, we believe that every individual deserves to be treated with dignity, respect, and compassion. Our goal is to create a community where our special angels feel valued, loved, and empowered to reach their full potential. We are committed to providing personalized care, tailored to meet the unique needs of each resident, ensuring they receive the support and encouragement they need to flourish.

Our dedicated team of caregivers, volunteers, and staff work tirelessly to create a warm and nurturing atmosphere. We offer a variety of programs and activities designed to enrich the lives of our residents, including spiritual services, recreational activities, educational opportunities, and social events. By fostering a sense of belonging and community, we aim to enhance the overall well-being of our angels, helping them to lead joyful and meaningful lives.

We invite you to join us in our mission to support and uplift our special angels. Whether through volunteering, donations, or simply spreading the word, your involvement makes a difference. Together, we can create a brighter future for our community and the extraordinary individuals we serve.

Plans Are In The Works!

We have the land to build the home and ministry center for our special needs angels who are able to care for themselves independently, but require supervision and some assistance.

The purpose of the home and ministry center is to not only provide a safe and loving home for our angels, but to also create a spiritual center for spiritual enjoyment full of worship, fellowship, activities, trips, and fun. We are committed to creating a nurturing and enriching environment for all who come through our doors.

Our Location
